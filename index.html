Dear Kurnia, Lica, Salwa & Aca ❤️

Honestly, I don't even know where to start.

Life moves so fast. People come and go. But somehow, you girls became one of the best things that ever happened to me.

Thank you for every random conversation, every inside joke, every laugh that made our stomachs hurt, and every moment that turned into a memory I'll never forget.

We've seen each other being happy, stressed, dramatic, confused, and completely clueless about life. Yet somehow, we're still here, still choosing each other.

Maybe one day we'll be busy chasing different dreams and living different lives. But I hope no matter how far life takes us, we'll always find our way back to each other.

Because some friendships are more than just friendship.

They become home.

So if any of you ever forget how much you mean to me, remember this: you are loved, appreciated, and a part of my heart forever.

Thank you for being my people.

Love always,
Dinda ❤️
